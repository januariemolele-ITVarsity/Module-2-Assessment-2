FIRST NATIONAL BANK


First National Bank is one of South Africa's "big five" banks, an authorised financial services and credit provider and also South Africa's oldest bank. It is a division of FirstRand, a large financial services conglomorate, which trades on the JSE, under the symbol: FSR. FNB is one of the three major divisions of the FirstRand Group, it maintains banking subsidiaries which it owns wholly or in part, in Botswana, Mozambique, Namibia, South Africa, Eswatini, Tanzania, Zambia, Ghana, India, Lesotho, and Guernsey.
